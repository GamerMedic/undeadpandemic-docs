# ðŸŒ¾ Tainted Crops

Information about tainted crops in UndeadPandemic.

> **Note:** Tainted crops is a planned feature and has **not been implemented** yet.

---

### What Happens?

- When you farm any crop grown in soil, there is a **chance** that you will receive a **tainted version** of the crop.
- Eating food produced from tainted crops will **cause an infection**.
- The food will still **replenish the player normally**, despite being tainted.

---

### Identifying Tainted Crops

- Tainted crops will include the words **"Tainted"** or **"Withered"** in their name.

---

### Modifying Tainted Crops

- The chance for crops to become tainted can be configured in the `config.yml` file.
- Tainted crops can be **completely disabled** via the `config.yml` file.
