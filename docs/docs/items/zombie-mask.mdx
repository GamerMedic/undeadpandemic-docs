## Description
Zombie Masks are hats that **disguise the player from zombies**, preventing zombies from attacking.

## What Do Zombie Masks Do?
- Zombie Masks make the player **hidden from zombies**.
- They are worn as a hat.
- Masks have a **set durability** which reduces over time as the rotting flesh falls away.
- Masks are **washed away completely** in rain or when swimming.

## How Are Zombie Masks Obtained?
- Zombie Masks can be obtained **at random when killing a zombie with a sword**.
- The `undeadpandemic.use.zombie` permission is required to use Zombie Masks.

## Modifying Zombie Masks
- Zombie Masks **cannot be modified**.
